Your environment shapes your thinking. Design it intentionally.

## The Principle

Every physical object in your workspace either adds energy or drains it. Clutter creates cognitive load. A clean desk creates mental space.

## My Setup

**Desk** — As empty as possible. Only what I'm working on right now.

**Monitors** — One large screen. Multiple monitors fragment attention.

**Lighting** — Natural light in the morning. Warm artificial light in the evening. This matters more than I expected.

**Sound** — Either silence or instrumental music. No words.

## The Digital Equivalent

Physical minimalism doesn't work if your digital environment is chaos. Clean desktop, limited notifications, thoughtful folder structure.

## The ROI

A few hours spent designing your workspace pays back many times over. You spend most of your waking life there. Make it count.
