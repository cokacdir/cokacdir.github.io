The fastest learners aren't smarter — they just have better feedback loops.

## The Loop

Learn → Apply → Get feedback → Adjust → Repeat

Every step matters. But most people skip the middle — they learn without applying, or apply without getting real feedback.

## Deliberate Practice

There's a difference between doing something 10,000 times and practicing deliberately for 1,000 hours. Repetition alone doesn't produce mastery. Focused, effortful practice does.

## Techniques That Work

**Spaced repetition** — Review at increasing intervals. The forgetting curve is real; fight it.

**The Feynman Technique** — Explain what you learned as if teaching a child. Gaps in understanding become immediately obvious.

**Building in public** — Write about what you're learning. Teaching forces you to know the material at a deeper level.

## The Meta-skill

Learning how to learn is the most valuable skill of all. Invest in your learning process, not just the content you're learning.
