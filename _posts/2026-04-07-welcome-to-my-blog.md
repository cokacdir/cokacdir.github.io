Welcome to my corner of the internet.

This blog is where I share things that matter to me — ideas, experiments, observations, and anything else worth writing down.

## Why I Started Writing

Writing forces clarity. When you can't explain something simply, you don't understand it well enough. This blog is my attempt to understand things better.

## What to Expect

- **Tech & Ideas** — Things I'm building or thinking about
- **Notes** — Short thoughts and observations  
- **Essays** — Longer pieces on topics I care about

Let's get started.
